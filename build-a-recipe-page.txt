  <h1>Chocolate Chip Cookies</h1>
  <p>This classic recipe will guide you through making delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies that are crispy on the edges and chewy in the center.</p>

  <h2>Ingredients</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour</li>
    <li>1 teaspoon baking soda</li>
    <li>1 cup butter, softened</li>
    <li>3/4 cup brown sugar</li>
    <li>3/4 cup white sugar</li>
    <li>2 large eggs</li>
    <li>2 cups chocolate chips</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Instructions</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).</li>
    <li>Mix flour and baking soda in a bowl.</li>
    <li>Cream together butter and sugars until smooth.</li>
    <li>Add eggs and mix well, then stir in dry ingredients.</li>
    <li>Fold in chocolate chips.</li>
    <li>Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.</li>
    <li>Bake for 8–10 minutes until golden brown.</li>
  </ol>
